Resources
===========

Some of the used textures and models were created by Nobiax (http://nobiax.deviantart.com/).

Some fantastic music was created by Chris Zabriskie (http://chriszabriskie.com/).
The song "The Temperature of the Air on the Bow of the Kaleetan" (background music in the game) is from his album "Undercover Vampire Policeman".
He shares his songs via the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 license. 
The conditions of that license can be found under http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
